What We Do

GeckoAdvisor is a free, privacy-respecting website scanner that analyzes websites for privacy issues, cookies, trackers, and third-party requests. We provide a deterministic privacy score with explainable evidence to help you understand how websites collect and use data.

By using our service, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you submit a scan request:

  • Website URL you want to analyze
  • Timestamp of the scan request
  • Cloudflare Turnstile challenge token (for bot protection)

When you create an optional account:

  • Email address (for authentication and scan history access)
  • Password (encrypted with industry-standard hashing)

Note: Accounts are optional. You can use our service anonymously without creating an account.

When you contact us:

  • Email address
  • Message content and support requests

Information Collected Automatically

Scan results we generate:

  • Privacy score and category breakdowns
  • Evidence of trackers, cookies, and third-party requests found on scanned websites
  • HTTP headers and security findings
  • Metadata about the scan (duration, pages analyzed, etc.)

Technical information:

  • IP address (used temporarily for rate limiting and abuse prevention)
  • User agent (browser type and version)

Note: We do NOT use analytics services or tracking cookies. We do NOT track you across websites.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

✓
Provide our service — Process scan requests, generate privacy reports, and display results
✓
Maintain public benefit — Store and display recent scan results to help everyone understand website privacy practices
✓
Prevent abuse — Use Cloudflare Turnstile to prevent bot abuse and excessive automated requests
✓
Communicate with you — Respond to support requests and provide service updates
✓
Comply with laws — Meet legal and regulatory obligations

We do NOT use your data for marketing, advertising, or selling to third parties.

Scan reports are publicly accessible via a shareable URL. This transparency helps everyone understand website privacy practices. Do not submit URLs containing sensitive or private information.

Data Security

We protect your data with:

  • Encryption (HTTPS/TLS for transmission)
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt with salt)
  • Bot protection via Cloudflare Turnstile
  • Limited access to production data
  • Regular security updates

While we take security seriously, no system is 100% secure. If you suspect a breach, contact us immediately at support@privacygecko.com.
