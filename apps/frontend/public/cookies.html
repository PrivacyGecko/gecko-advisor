What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and provide a better experience.

For more details about our data practices, see our Privacy Policy.

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Prevent bot abuse and automated attacks (Cloudflare Turnstile)
  • Keep you logged in (if you create an optional account)
  • Ensure fair access for all users

We do NOT use cookies for:

  • Analytics or usage tracking
  • Advertising or marketing
  • Tracking you across websites
  • Profiling or behavioral analysis
